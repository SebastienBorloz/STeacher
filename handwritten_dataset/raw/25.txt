Ex 3

\[
P(\text{il fait beau}) = \frac{3}{5}
\]

\[
P(B) = \frac{3}{5} \quad P(H) = \frac{2}{5}
\]

\[
P(H|B) = 0.8
\]

\[
P(M| PH) = 0.25
\]

a) 
\[
P(H) = P(H|B) \cdot P(B) + P(H|\overline{B}) \cdot P(\overline{B})
\]

\[
= 0.8 \cdot 0.6 + 0.25 \cdot (1 - 0.6)
\]

\[
= 0.58
\]

b) 
\[
P(B|H) = \frac{P(H|B) \cdot P(B)}{P(H)}
\]

\[
= \frac{0.8 \cdot 0.6}{0.58} = 0.8
\]
